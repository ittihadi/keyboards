After the relative success of the switch plate, I moved on to making the other parts,
mainly a plate that's going to be in place of a PCB, a case, and a cover for the
microcontroller.

## The "PCB"

This turned out to be the most convoluted part I'd be making, as I was inspired by
the [Handwired Sofle]() I saw on reddit, I wanted to do a similar thing and make my
substitute PCB have grooves for the wires of the LED as well as slots for the LEDs
themselves and the switch sockets.

For the LEDs and Sockets it turned out to be relatively simple as I was able to find
the specifications for both online, as well as the holes for the switches not included
as part of the socket.

As for the wire guides, I was actually able to find some bezier curve functions, though
they ended up slowing down the model rendering quite a bit
